Our Story

Founded in 2018, Noir Atelier represents the intersection of minimalist design and exceptional craftsmanship. Each piece is conceived in our Paris atelier and brought to life by artisans who share our commitment to timeless elegance.

We believe in the power of restraint — that true luxury lies not in excess, but in the careful selection of materials, the precision of cut, and the quiet confidence of understated design.
