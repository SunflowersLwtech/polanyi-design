# Credits & References

This project is an original work, not a fork. It draws inspiration and theoretical
grounding from the following sources, which we gratefully acknowledge.

## Theoretical Foundation

**Michael Polanyi** (1891–1976)
- *Personal Knowledge: Towards a Post-Critical Philosophy* (1958)
- *The Tacit Dimension* (1966)
- Core concepts applied: subsidiary-focal awareness, from-to integration, indwelling,
  gestalt integration, tacit knowing, post-critical judgment

**Hubert & Stuart Dreyfus** — Skill acquisition model (5 stages: novice → expert)
- *Mind Over Machine* (1986)

**Harry Collins** — Tacit knowledge taxonomy (relational, somatic, collective)
- *Tacit and Explicit Knowledge* (2010)

**Gestalt Psychology** (Wertheimer, Köhler, Koffka) — Perceptual integration principles
that Polanyi drew heavily from and that map directly to visual design.

## Inspirations

**[enzyme2013/polanyi-skill](https://github.com/enzyme2013/polanyi-skill)** (MIT License)
- The first public attempt to distill Polanyi's thinking into a Claude Code skill.
- Built with [alchaincyf/nuwa-skill](https://github.com/alchaincyf/nuwa-skill).
- Our work was inspired by their approach of encoding a thinker's cognitive patterns
  as operational frameworks, but takes a different direction: we focus specifically on
  frontend design and code, and operate as a cognitive enhancement layer rather than
  a role-play system.

**Google Design MD (Stitch)**
- The structured markdown format for design system specifications influenced our
  approach to encoding design knowledge in machine-readable markdown.
- Reference: https://stitch.withgoogle.com/docs/design-md/format

## Academic Context

**David Autor** — "Polanyi's Paradox and the Shape of Employment Growth" (NBER, 2014)
- Named the paradox that motivates this work: expert knowledge resists codification.

**Ikujiro Nonaka & Hirotaka Takeuchi** — *The Knowledge-Creating Company* (1995)
- SECI model. We note that their treatment of tacit→explicit "conversion" diverges from
  Polanyi's original position that tacit knowledge structurally resists full articulation.

**The Polanyi Society** — https://polanyisociety.org/
- Ongoing scholarly community for Polanyi studies.
